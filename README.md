# 🌫️ The Lornsmouth Horror

> *A deeply branching Lovecraftian horror interactive fiction game set in a cursed 1920s New England village.*

## Overview

**The Lornsmouth Horror** is an atmospheric text-based adventure game where every choice matters. You arrive at the fog-shrouded village of Lornsmouth only to discover an ancient cosmic horror awakening beneath the waves. Your decisions determine not only your survival, but your very sanity.

---

## 🎮 Features

### Deep Branching Narrative
- **43 unique story steps** with interconnected paths
- **100+ decision points** that meaningfully affect the story
- **Multiple routes** to each ending based on your choices
- **Dynamic storytelling** that responds to your investigation style

### Sanity System
- Start with **100 Sanity points**
- Each choice costs **5-25 sanity** depending on its psychological toll
- Real-time sanity meter with visual feedback
- Status changes: Rational → Unsettled → Disturbed → Terrified → Breaking → Shattered
- Automatic madness ending if sanity reaches 0

### Four Distinct Endings

1. **💀 Embrace of Madness** - Your mind shatters and you join the cult
2. **⛵ Haunted Escape** - Survive but forever traumatized  
3. **🛡️ The Eternal Guardian** - Seal the entity and become its watcher
4. **♾️ Transcendence** - Master dark powers and transcend humanity

### Atmospheric Design
- **1920s Lovecraftian aesthetic** with period-appropriate typography
- **Film grain overlay** for vintage horror feel
- **Pixel art style** imagery for each scene (128x128)
- **Color-coded UI** that responds to your mental state
- **Smooth transitions** and visual feedback for choices

## 🗺️ Story Paths

### 🔍 The Investigation Path
Research the mystery, gather evidence, and use knowledge as your weapon. Explore:
- Captain's logs with impossible dates
- Forbidden library archives  
- Ancient symbols and their meanings
- The conspiracy of the three families

**Best for:** Players who value lore and want to understand the horror before confronting it

---

### ⛪ The Conspiracy Path
Interact with the corrupted townspeople and uncover the web of lies. Discover:
- The priest's dark warnings
- Centuries-old bloodline secrets
- Desecrated holy places
- Hidden shrines to the deep ones

**Best for:** Players who enjoy social manipulation and uncovering secrets

---

### 🎭 The Infiltration Path
Blend in with the cultists to learn their plans from within. Experience:
- Ritual mask acquisition and meaning
- Joining the forbidden processions
- Witnessing the cult's inner circle
- Stealing the ritual tome for power

**Best for:** Risk-takers who want to get dangerously close to the truth

---

### ⚔️ The Combat Path
Face the horror head-on with courage and steel. Features:
- Direct confrontation with cosmic entities
- Acquiring weapons ("against men, not gods")
- Disrupting the summoning ritual
- Fighting for pyrrhic victory

**Best for:** Players who prefer direct action over investigation

---

### 🌊 The Deep Exploration Path  
Venture into the ancient places beneath the village. Uncover:
- Phosphorescent caves with grotesque carvings
- Underwater passages to pre-human ruins
- The sealed Deep Temple
- Artifacts of binding and sealing

**Best for:** Explorers seeking the deepest lore and cosmic revelations

## 💡 Strategy Tips

### Sanity Management
- **Low-cost choices (5-10):** Basic exploration, reading, following
- **Medium-cost (10-15):** Supernatural investigation, confrontation
- **High-cost (20-25):** Combat, cosmic visions, dark rituals

You can typically afford **2-3 high-cost choices** if balanced with safer options.

### Path Recommendations

**First Playthrough:** Investigation → Research → Guardian ending
- Safest route with moderate sanity costs
- Unlocks the most lore
- Most "heroic" conclusion

**Second Playthrough:** Infiltration → Tome → Transcendence ending  
- High risk, high reward
- Requires mastering forbidden knowledge
- Most unique ending

**Third Playthrough:** Pure survival → Escape ending
- Minimal investigation
- Focus on staying alive
- Classic horror survival experience

**Fourth Playthrough:** Embrace the horror → Madness ending
- Make high-cost choices deliberately
- Seek cosmic revelations
- Experience total breakdown

### Hidden Discoveries
- The village itself forms a massive summoning sigil (visible from rooftops)
- The lighthouse keeper is wired into the ritual mechanism
- The entity predates human civilization
- Some paths reveal time-loop implications

## 🎯 How to Play

1. **Read each scene carefully** - Details matter in cosmic horror
2. **Consider sanity costs** - Can you afford this revelation?
3. **Make your choice** - Click one of the presented options
4. **Watch your sanity** - The meter changes color as you deteriorate
5. **Reach an ending** - Four possible fates await
6. **Play again** - Discover different paths and endings

### Controls
- **Click choice buttons** to make decisions
- **Scroll** to read longer scenes
- **Click "Begin Again"** at endings to restart
