# 🎮 Quick Start Guide

## First-Time Players: Start Here!

Welcome to **The Lornsmouth Horror**! Here's everything you need to know in 2 minutes.

---

## 🎯 Your Goal

**Survive the night in Lornsmouth with your sanity intact.**

You arrive at a cursed village on the night of a ritual summoning. Your choices determine whether you:
- Escape alive but haunted
- Seal the entity as an eternal guardian  
- Master dark powers and transcend
- Lose your mind completely

---

## 📊 The Sanity System

### How It Works
- You start with **100 sanity points**
- Every choice costs **5 to 25 points**
- Your mental state changes as sanity drops:
  - **80-100:** Rational (green)
  - **60-79:** Unsettled (yellow-green)
  - **40-59:** Disturbed (yellow, pulsing)
  - **20-39:** Terrified (orange, pulsing)  
  - **1-19:** Breaking (red, intense pulsing)
  - **0:** Shattered (automatic madness ending)

### Strategy
- **Safe path:** Mostly 5-10 cost choices = ~10-12 steps before ending
- **Risky path:** Several 20+ cost choices = 6-8 steps before ending  
- **Balance:** Mix of costs = 8-10 steps with more options

**TIP:** You can usually afford 2-3 high-cost revelations if you choose carefully elsewhere.

## 🗺️ Recommended First Playthrough

### "The Cautious Investigator"

**Starting Choice:** Search the docked vessels for clues (5 sanity)

**Strategy:**
- Gather information before confronting danger
- Visit the library, study symbols, read journals
- Avoid direct combat with entities
- Learn about the ritual to disrupt it later

**Likely Ending:** Guardian (seal the entity) or Escape (flee alive)

**Total Sanity Cost:** ~80-90 points (you'll survive with 10-20 left)

### Specific Path Example
1. **Arrival** → Search vessels (5)
2. **Sailor's Log** → Take evidence (10)  
3. **Alley** → Follow light (5)
4. **Town Square** → Enter library (5)
5. **Library** → Study ritual book (20)
6. **Knowledge** → Prepare for confrontation (10)
7. **Storm** → Approach lighthouse (15)
8. **Summoning** → Perform counter-ritual (20)
9. **Counter-Ritual** → Complete banishment (25)
10. **END:** Guardian (finished with ~15 sanity)

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

### Don't Do This:
❌ **Choosing every high-cost option** → Quick madness  
❌ **Ignoring the sanity meter** → Surprise bad ending  
❌ **Random clicking** → Miss important lore  
❌ **Always fleeing** → Boring path, fewer options  

### Do This Instead:
✅ **Read scenes carefully** → Details unlock better choices later  
✅ **Balance risk and reward** → Some revelations are worth 20+ sanity  
✅ **Watch the meter color** → Visual warning system  
✅ **Explore different areas** → Variety prevents getting stuck

## 💡 Gameplay Tips

1. **First choice matters** - Three different starting paths
2. **Town square is central** - Many paths converge here
3. **The lighthouse is the endgame** - Most paths lead there eventually
4. **Weapons help vs. cultists, not gods** - Choose your battles
5. **Knowledge is power** - But also dangerous to acquire
6. **Some endings require specific items** - Tome, key, or knowledge

---

## 📍 Key Decision Points

### Early Game (Steps 1-10)
**Question:** Investigate or flee?
- **Investigate** → More lore, better endings, higher cost
- **Flee** → Safer, fewer options, quicker ending

### Mid Game (Steps 11-20)  
**Question:** Confront or infiltrate?
- **Confront** → Direct action, combat path
- **Infiltrate** → Stealth, knowledge path

### Late Game (Steps 21+)
**Question:** Fight, seal, flee, or transcend?
- **Fight** → Pyrrhic victory → Escape/Guardian
- **Seal** → Complete ritual → Guardian  
- **Flee** → Survival → Escape
- **Transcend** → Master tome → Transcendence

## 🏆 Four Endings Explained

### 💀 Madness (Easy to get accidentally!)
- Sanity reaches 0
- OR choose to embrace horror
- **You become one of them**

### ⛵ Escape (Default "good" ending)
- Survive with sanity > 0
- Flee the village
- **You're alive but traumatized forever**

### 🛡️ Guardian (Heroic ending)
- Research the ritual thoroughly  
- OR find the Deep Temple key
- Perform binding ritual
- **You seal the entity and watch over it**

### ♾️ Transcendence (Secret ending)
- Steal ritual tome from cult
- Master its forbidden power
- Embrace transformation  
- **You become something beyond human**
