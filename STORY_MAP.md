# The Lornsmouth Horror - Story Map

## Overview
An expanded Lovecraftian horror game with **43 story steps** and **4 possible endings**, featuring deeply branching narrative paths based on player choices and sanity management.

---

## Starting Paths (Step 1)

From the fog-shrouded dock, three initial choices:

1. **Approach the crowd** → Strange Greeting (Step 2)
2. **Sneak into alley** → Fogbound Alley (Step 3)  
3. **Search vessels** → The Sailor's Log (Step 22) *[NEW]*

---

## Major Story Branches

### 🔍 Investigation Path
**Steps:** 22, 24, 25, 27, 29, 31
- Find evidence in captain's logs
- Discover village's occult layout from rooftops
- Research in forbidden library
- Gain knowledge about the ritual and entity
- Study ancient symbols and family histories

**Key Features:**
- Lower sanity costs overall
- Unlocks counter-ritual options later
- Can lead to Guardian or Transcendence endings

---

### ⛪ Religious/Conspiracy Path  
**Steps:** 6, 23, 26, 28, 30
- Interact with the corrupted priest
- Learn about the three ruling bloodlines
- Discover desecrated church
- Find innkeeper's shrine to deep ones
- Uncover the full scope of the conspiracy

**Key Features:**
- Medium sanity costs
- Social manipulation options
- Can infiltrate or expose the cult

---

### 🎭 Infiltration Path
**Steps:** 11, 14, 16, 31, 33
- Acquire and study the ritual masks
- Join the cultist procession
- Get close to the actual summoning
- Can steal the ritual tome for power

**Key Features:**
- Risky but informative
- Access to inner cult secrets
- Can lead to sabotage or joining them

---

### ⚔️ Combat/Direct Path
**Steps:** 5, 32, 34, 37, 41
- Fight the creature in the alley
- Obtain weapons from innkeeper
- Climb lighthouse exterior
- Perform counter-ritual
- Achieve pyrrhic victory

**Key Features:**
- Highest sanity costs
- Most dramatic confrontations
- Direct disruption of ritual

---

### 🌊 Deep Exploration Path
**Steps:** 17, 30, 36, 40
- Explore caves beneath cliffs
- Find underwater passages
- Discover the Deep Temple
- Encounter the sealed altar

**Key Features:**
- Uncover ancient pre-human ruins
- High sanity cost but reveals deepest lore
- Access to sealing/binding options

## The Four Endings

### 1. 💀 Embrace of Madness (Step 20)
**How to reach:** 
- Let sanity drop to 0
- Choose "Collapse from terror" (Step 5)
- Choose "Welcome the revelation" (Step 9)
- Choose "Stand paralyzed in awe" (Step 18)
- Choose "Face whatever follows" (Step 38)

**Outcome:** Your mind shatters. You join the cult willingly, finally understanding your true purpose.

---

### 2. ⛵ Haunted Escape (Step 21)
**How to reach:**
- Survive with sanity above 0
- Choose escape options from Step 19, 38, or 41
- Don't complete binding rituals

**Outcome:** You flee Lornsmouth as it collapses. You survived, but the nightmares will never end.

---

### 3. 🛡️ The Eternal Guardian (Step 42) *[NEW]*
**How to reach:**
- Gain knowledge through research (Steps 25, 27, 29)
- Discover the Deep Temple (Step 40) and take the key
- OR perform the complete counter-ritual (Step 37 → 42)
- Maintain moderate-to-high sanity

**Outcome:** You seal the entity for another age. You become the guardian who remembers, watching over the world against future threats.

**Requirements:** Must have studied the symbols/rituals OR found the temple key

---

### 4. ♾️ Transcendence (Step 43) *[NEW]*
**How to reach:**
- Steal the ritual tome (Step 16 → 35)
- Master its power (Step 35 → 39)
- Embrace your transformation (Step 39 → 43)

**Outcome:** You transcend humanity, becoming something between mortal and cosmic horror. You reshape Lornsmouth according to your new will.

**Requirements:** Must obtain and master the ritual tome. High risk, high sanity cost.

## Critical Choice Points

### Knowledge vs. Action
- **Research Path** (Steps 25, 27): Lower immediate danger, unlocks powerful options later
- **Direct Confrontation** (Steps 32, 34): Higher risk, quicker resolution

### Trust vs. Paranoia
- **Trust the Priest** (Step 6): Potential ally or trap?
- **Investigate Everyone** (Steps 30, 31): Uncover betrayals but increase isolation

### Power vs. Humanity
- **Destroy the Tome** (Step 35): Remain human, lose power
- **Master the Tome** (Step 39): Gain power, lose humanity

### Fight vs. Flight
- **Disrupt Ritual** (Step 37): Direct confrontation, chance for victory
- **Escape** (Step 19): Survival, but no resolution

## Sanity Management Tips

**Low Cost Actions (5-10 points):**
- Basic exploration
- Reading journals
- Following processions
- Standard interactions

**Medium Cost Actions (10-15 points):**
- Investigating supernatural elements
- Confronting cultists
- Studying forbidden knowledge

**High Cost Actions (20-25 points):**
- Direct combat with entities
- Deep cosmic revelations
- Mastering dark powers
- Witnessing the summoning

**Strategy:** 
- Start with 100 sanity
- Most paths require 8-12 choices to reach an ending
- Average cost per choice: 10-15 points
- You can afford 2-3 high-cost choices if balanced with low-cost ones
- Reaching 0 sanity = automatic Madness ending

## Unique Story Discoveries

### Hidden Lore
- **Captain's Log** (Step 22): Dated today—implies time loop or prophecy
- **Village Sigil** (Step 24): The entire town is a summoning circle
- **Three Families** (Step 26): Centuries-old bloodline conspiracy
- **Keeper's Corpse** (Step 34): The lighthouse keeper is part of the ritual mechanism
- **Pre-Human Ruins** (Step 40): The entity predates humanity

### Special Items/Knowledge
- **Photographs of Symbols** (Step 27): Evidence and ritual disruption knowledge
- **Harpoon & Silver Bullets** (Step 32): "Against men, not gods"
- **Ritual Tome** (Step 35): Path to transcendence
- **Temple Key** (Step 40): Binding/sealing tool
