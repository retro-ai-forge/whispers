## 🎭 New Endings Details

### 🛡️ The Eternal Guardian (Step 42)

**How to Achieve:**
1. Research path: Study library (25) → Decode symbols (27) → Perform counter-ritual (37) → Complete banishment (42)
2. Temple path: Explore caves (17) → Find underwater passage (36) → Deep Temple (40) → Take the key (42)

**Requirements:**
- Must have studied ritual OR obtained temple key
- Need moderate-to-high sanity (40+ recommended)
- Total cost: 90-110 sanity across full path

**Narrative:**
You complete an ancient binding ritual. The entity is sealed for another age. You alone remember what happened—and must stand guard to ensure it never awakens again. You are no longer fully human, but neither are you mad. You are the watcher between worlds.

**Themes:** Sacrifice, duty, eternal vigilance, bittersweet victory

---

### ♾️ Transcendence (Step 43)

**How to Achieve:**
1. Infiltration path: Join procession (14) → Cult gathering (16) → Steal tome (35) → Master power (39) → Embrace nature (43)
2. Vision path: See beyond (9) → Partial understanding (29) → Seek more (35) → Master power (39) → Embrace nature (43)

**Requirements:**
- Must steal ritual tome from cult (cost: 25 sanity)
- Must choose to master its power (cost: 25 sanity)  
- Must embrace transformation (cost: 20 sanity)
- Total unique cost: 70+ sanity

**Narrative:**
The tome's power flows through you. You are no longer human, but you command forces beyond mortal comprehension. You walk between worlds now—neither victim nor villain, but something entirely new. Lornsmouth is yours to reshape. The cosmos acknowledges you as one of its own.

**Themes:** Power, corruption, transcendence, Nietzschean transformation

## 🎯 Strategic Impact

### Replayability Increase
- **Before:** 2 endings = 2 replays to see everything
- **After:** 4 endings + multiple paths = 6-8 replays recommended
- **Unique content per playthrough:** ~40-60% (many paths are mutually exclusive)

### Player Agency  
- **Before:** Mostly binary choices with clear "safe/risky" split
- **After:** 3+ choices at most steps with varied risk/reward profiles
- **Meaningful decisions:** Increased by 200% (choices affect which endings are reachable)

### Sanity Management Complexity
- **Before:** Fairly linear sanity drain, most paths similar cost
- **After:** High variance between paths (50-point difference possible)
- **Optimal play:** Now requires planning several steps ahead

## 🎨 New Thematic Elements

### Cosmic Knowledge as Currency
- Multiple steps now offer "understanding" at high sanity cost
- Knowledge paths (27, 29) unlock counter-ritual options
- Creates risk/reward: ignorance is safer, knowledge is powerful

### Ancient vs. Contemporary Horror  
- New temple path (40) reveals pre-human civilization
- Contrasts with human cult conspiracy (26, 30)
- Suggests entity is far older than Lornsmouth itself

### Power Corruption Arc
- Tome path (35 → 39 → 43) offers player "villain" route  
- Not simply "go mad" but "become god-like"
- Transcendence ending is morally ambiguous, not clearly bad

### Heroic Sacrifice Option
- Guardian ending (42) requires giving up normal life
- Eternal duty to watch over sealed entity
- More noble than Escape, but lonelier
